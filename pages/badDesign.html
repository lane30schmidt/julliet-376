On November 11th, 2006 Sony released the PS3 which showcased a familiar homescreen UI that they still use today in 2022 with their most recent console being the PS5. Towards the top of the screen we see a bar of games that are organized by most recently used going left to right.


Here is an example of what the home screen of a PS5 looks like today.



When it comes to the PS5 home screen, and specifically the game bar, there really is not any form of organization provided to the user. The only setting we have is the ability to prevent a game from leaving the home screen. So I have put together a prototype solution to give the user a little more customization.
